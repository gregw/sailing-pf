Historical Performance Factors

The HPF project provides an online database of elapsed times and time corrected factors (TCF) from Australian keelboat sail racing, with statistical analysis and graphical presentation. Its goals include:

  • Time correction factors (TCF) that apply over the entire Australian keelboat racing fleet
  • Handicap calculators to support conversion between handicap systems
  • Experimentation with alternate ways to evaluate performance

The factors derived include:

  • Reference Factor (RF) - A design-centric TCF deterministically calculated from measurement certificates and/or from results against other boats/designs with RFs.
  • Historical Performance Factor (HPF) - A performance-centric TCF determined by an optimisation process that looks at historical performance.
  • Performance Profile (PP) - A value that rates a boats overall performance by combining frequency, diversity, consistency, stability and chaotic tendencies.
