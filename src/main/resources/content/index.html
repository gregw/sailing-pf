Historical Performance Factors

The HPF project provides an online database of elapsed times and time corrected factors from Australian sail racing, with statistical analysis and graphical presentation. Its goals include:

  • Time correction factors that apply over the entire Australian keelboat racing fleet
  • Handicap calculators to support conversion between handicap systems
  • Experimentation with alternate ways to evaluate performance
